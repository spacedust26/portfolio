* ### Multi-Regional Medicinal Plant Leaf Identification using Explainable Deep Learning with Attention Mechanism
  Under review in Journal of Ayurveda and Integrative Medicine

  This study presents a multi-regional, large-scale medicinal plant leaf identification framework that addresses dataset imbalance and diversity through a curated collection of 145 species and 29,000 images. Attention-enhanced deep learning models achieve near-perfect accuracy, with explainable AI techniques providing interpretability and reinforcing trust in automated plant identification systems.

  **Keywords:** Plant Leaf Classification, Deep Learning, Attention Mechanism, Explainable AI, Multi-Regional Dataset, Sustainable Development Goals

* ### An Explainable Multi-Model AI Framework for Early Detection of Sickle Cell Anemia from Blood Smear Images

  This study aims to systematically review existing machine learning and deep learning approaches for sickle cell anemia detection, evaluate and compare data-driven models across multiple datasets, and analyze their performance, efficiency, and generalization capabilities. Additionally, the work emphasizes interpretability through explainable AI techniques, providing both quantitative and qualitative insights into model behavior, reliability, and practical deployment considerations.

  **Keywords:** deep learning, explainability, machine learning, sickle cell anemia detection, sustainable development goals
